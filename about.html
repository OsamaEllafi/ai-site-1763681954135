About English Prep

Your ultimate resource for mastering English grammar and vocabulary for high school exams.

Our Mission

English Prep is designed to provide high school students with an interactive and effective way to prepare for their English final exams. We believe that consistent practice and clear explanations are key to building confidence and achieving excellent results.

Key Features

  • Targeted Grammar Practice: Focus on crucial areas like tenses, prepositions, and sentence structure.
  • Extensive Vocabulary Building: Learn academic words, literary terms, synonyms, and antonyms.
  • Interactive Quizzes: Test your knowledge with immediate feedback and detailed results.
  • Dynamic Flashcards: Reinforce learning with customizable flashcards and 'learned' tracking.

Progress Tracking (Local Storage)

We understand the importance of tracking your progress. To keep things simple and private, English Prep utilizes your browser's local storage. This means:

  • Your quiz scores and flashcard 'learned' status are saved directly in your browser.
  • No signup or login required â€“ your data stays on your device.
  • You can continue where you left off on the same device.

How to manage your data:

If you wish to clear your progress for a specific topic, you can often do so within the quiz/flashcard page itself (e.g., a 'Reset Progress' button). Alternatively, you can clear all local storage data for this website through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Site Settings > View permissions and data stored across sites > Search for this site and clear its data.
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data > Manage Data... > Search for this site and Remove Selected.
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data... > Search for this site and Remove.

Please note that clearing local storage will remove all your saved progress on this site.
